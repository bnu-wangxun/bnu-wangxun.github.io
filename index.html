Xun Wang

Xun Wang is currently an algorithm engineer at Malong Technologies . He obtained his M.S. of Probability and Statistics in school of mathematics of Sun Yat-Sen University in 2018.

Before that, he got his B.S. degree of Math and Applied Math from Beijing Normal University.
